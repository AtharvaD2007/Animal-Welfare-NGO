Exposés and Undercover Investigations

entities have conducted more undercover investigations than any other animal group. They undertake investigations to expose animal abuse in laboratories, on farms and in slaughterhouses, in the clothing industry, in the pet trade, and elsewhere.

efforts have led to significant victories, including the closure of abusive laboratories and farms as well as the seizure and rescue of suffering animals.

has been launching investigations since 1981, beginning with the exposure of the suffering of the Silver Spring monkeys in a Maryland experimentation facility. This investigation resulted in the first-ever police raid on a laboratory, the first confiscation of abused animals from a laboratory, and the first U.S. Supreme Court victory for animals used in experiments.
